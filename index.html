Troubleshooting:

  • Make sure you're on HTTPS (URL starts with https://)
  • Look for camera icon in address bar and click "Allow"
  • Try different browsers (Chrome works best)
  • Don't use in-app browsers (Instagram, Facebook, etc.)
  • Some corporate networks block camera access
